Project goal

This project is to work with ChatGPT to create a non-partisan assessment of President Trump. Original ChatGPT prompt:

Please make a web page that is a list of the top 100 Trump accomplishments or impacts, both good and bad without judgement as to good or bad, ranked in descending order of impact. By impact, I'm, not sure, but maybe combination of money and number of people impacted? Examples would include deportation (legal vs. illegal vs. criminal/illegal), spending cuts or increases to science, art, health, welfare, etc. Tax changes, environment, climate change, deficit changes. Try to get a factual, non-partisan assessment. Add links for each item to both a conservative and liberal interpretation of the impact.

So far, there have been about 45 prompts and answers to create the current website. I may publish the entire transcript of the conversation in the future.

Why This Weighting?

The weighting balances population and economic effects with institutional permanence, duration, international impact, and historical significance. This avoids ranking only by dollar value and better captures actions such as court appointments, USAID restructuring, WHO withdrawal, PEPFAR changes, and foreign-policy shifts.

Source Recency Rule

For active or evolving topics such as tariffs, immigration enforcement, USAID restructuring, WHO withdrawal, and PEPFAR/global health changes, the site prioritizes recent sources. Older sources remain useful when they document original implementation, primary records, or foundational analysis.

Nonpartisan Standard

The site avoids declaring actions good or bad. Each entry presents what changed, how supporters interpret it, how critics interpret it, and why the action ranks where it does.
